Ear & Sinus -Stapedectomy
Stapedectomy is a surgical procedure carried out to replace the innermost bone (stapes) of the middle ear with prosthesis. This procedure is carried out to improve the progressive hearing loss in otosclerotic patients, a condition in which spongy bone hardens around the base of the stapes.
